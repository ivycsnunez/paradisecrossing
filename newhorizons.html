How I started

It was the start of the pandemic... March 2020 and coincidentally, Nintendo begun selling the newest Animal Crossing called New Horizons. We got our copies on day 1. It was also a time when I lost my job and I got severely sick (not Covid-19). So for me, it was meant to be. The game is calling out to me... to go back to the virtual islands and play. Looking back on my New Leaf post, it was like 2012 again, when I first tried the game.

How is it going?

Life happened. After getting better and the pandemic is still ongoing, I went back to work. The game took a backseat and I got to play every once in a while.
